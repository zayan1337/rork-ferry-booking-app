⚠️ Important Notice

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, you will lose access to:

  • All your personal data and settings
  • Purchase history and subscriptions
  • Saved content and preferences
  • Any premium features or benefits

How to Delete Your Account

To request account deletion, send an email to our support team with the following information:

  • Subject: Account Deletion Request
  • Email address associated with your account
  • Username (if applicable)
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

Processing Time: Account deletion requests are typically processed within 1-3 business days.

Data Retention Policy

Upon account deletion:

  • Personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Some data may be retained for legal compliance (transaction records, etc.)
  • Anonymous usage statistics may be retained for analytics
  • Backup systems may retain data for up to 90 days before permanent deletion
