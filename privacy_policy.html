1. Introduction

Welcome to Crystal Transfer Vaavu, a ferry ticketing application operated by Crystalline.MV Pvt Ltd (Reg. No. C-456/2009) ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services.

By using Crystal Transfer Vaavu, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you use our ferry ticketing service, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details, billing address (processed securely through payment providers)
  • Travel Information: Departure/arrival locations, travel dates, passenger details
  • Identity Verification: Government-issued ID details as required for ferry travel regulations
  • Contact Preferences: Communication preferences and language settings

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, features accessed, time spent in app
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates (with your permission) to show nearby ferry terminals
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, crash reports, performance data

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Ferry Operators

We share necessary booking and passenger information with ferry operators to facilitate your travel. This includes passenger names, travel details, and any special requirements.

4.2 Payment Processors

Payment information is processed by secure third-party payment providers (such as Stripe, PayPal) who handle transactions according to their own privacy policies.

4.3 Service Providers

We may share data with trusted service providers who help us operate our business, including:

  • Cloud hosting providers (AWS, Google Cloud)
  • Analytics providers (Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • Customer support platforms
  • Email and SMS service providers

4.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law, regulation, or legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or others.

We never sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: Data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Secure Storage: Personal data is stored on secure servers with access controls
  • Payment Security: We are PCI DSS compliant for payment card data handling
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Security Audits: Periodic security assessments and updates

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for different periods depending on the type of data:

  • Account Information: Until account deletion or 3 years of inactivity
  • Booking Records: 7 years for accounting and legal compliance
  • Payment Information: As required by payment processors and regulations
  • Usage Data: Up to 2 years for analytics and improvement purposes
  • Support Communications: 3 years for quality assurance

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at the information provided in Section 10.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by relevant authorities
  • Adequacy decisions by data protection authorities
  • Appropriate technical and organizational measures

9. Children's Privacy

Crystal Transfer Vaavu is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
